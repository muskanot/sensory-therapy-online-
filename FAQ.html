Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Online Occupational Therapy?

Online Occupational Therapy is a virtual therapy service where sessions are conducted through video calls to improve daily living, motor, sensory, and cognitive skills.

Who can take Online OT sessions?

Children with developmental delay, ADHD, Autism, sensory issues, handwriting difficulties, and adults needing rehabilitation can benefit.

Are online sessions effective?

Yes, online OT sessions are effective when conducted with proper guidance, parent involvement, and personalized therapy plans.
