What is Online Occupational Therapy?

Online Occupational Therapy is a structured and evidence-based therapy approach that helps children develop skills required for daily living, school participation and social interaction. Through guided virtual sessions, children receive personalized therapy programs while parents learn strategies to support their child at home.

Online OT is highly effective for pediatric conditions such as sensory processing difficulties, ADHD, autism spectrum disorder, handwriting difficulties, fine motor delays and developmental delays. It allows therapy to continue in a familiar home environment, increasing comfort, consistency and carry-over of skills.
