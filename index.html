What is Online Occupational Therapy?

Online Occupational Therapy is a structured therapeutic approach where a certified occupational therapist works with children and parents through secure online sessions. These sessions focus on improving a child’s ability to perform daily activities such as self-care, play, school tasks and social participation.

Online OT is especially beneficial for children who experience sensory processing difficulties, fine motor delays, handwriting problems, attention difficulties, developmental delays, autism spectrum disorder or ADHD. Parents are actively involved in sessions, making therapy practical and effective at home.

Why Choose Online Occupational Therapy for Your Child?

  • ✔ Convenient therapy sessions from the comfort of your home
  • ✔ Parent-guided therapy ensuring better carryover into daily routines
  • ✔ Personalized therapy plans based on your child’s needs
  • ✔ Evidence-based techniques for sensory, motor and behavioral challenges
  • ✔ Regular progress tracking and parent counseling
