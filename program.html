Therapy Programs

1. Pediatric Occupational Therapy Program

This program is designed for children who face challenges in daily activities such as self-care, play, school tasks, and social participation. Therapy sessions focus on improving independence, coordination, attention, and confidence through child-friendly and goal-oriented activities.

2. Sensory Integration Therapy Program

This program helps children who are sensitive or under-responsive to sensory input such as sound, touch, movement, or textures. Structured sensory activities are used to improve regulation, focus, behavior, and emotional control.

3. Fine Motor & Handwriting Development Program

Designed for children who have difficulty with writing, grasp, hand strength, or coordination. This program improves pencil control, hand-eye coordination, and classroom performance.

4. ADHD & Autism Support Program

This program supports children with ADHD and Autism by focusing on attention, behavior regulation, sensory needs, communication, and daily living skills using evidence-based occupational therapy strategies.

5. Adult Occupational Therapy Program

Therapy for adults focusing on daily function, work-life balance, stress management, motor coordination, and independence in personal and professional activities.
